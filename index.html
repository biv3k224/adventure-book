To The Love of My Life

Dear Mayalu,

I hope you are doing well. I just wanted to write to you with all these honesty in my heart.

Looking back, I realize how precious you are and how careless my mistake was. You gave so much love, trust, and effort into us, and I failed to value it the way you deserved. The truth is, the problem was never in you - it was in me.

I carry a deep regret for hurting someone as beautiful as you - not just in looks, but in heart, mind and soul. You are rare, and you are precious. I wish I could have shown you more clearly how amazing you are through my eyes.

I want you to know that your happiness and success will always mean everything to me. No matter where life takes you, I'll always silently wish the best for you - because loving you taught me what real love actually feels like.

This mistake has changed me. It woke me up to parts of myself that I needed to fix, and I am growing because of it. I'm becoming a better man - not just for you, but for the future, for myself, and for anyone I may ever care about.

If somewhere in your heart, you still believe in us, if there's even a small space left for me, I would be grateful for the chance to build trust again - slowly, patiently, and truly this time.(Haule Haule)

But if not, I understand. And I will carry the love and lessons you gave me with respect and gratitude, always. Take care, my love. You are, and always will be, unforgettable to me.

With all my my Heart, Forever Yours,

Bibek Kumar Tamang
